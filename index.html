Welcome

This website serves as a brief CV, with a little bit about me, my skills, and achievements.

Outside of University I enjoy reading, spending time in the outdoors, and playing music by myself or with friends.

Achievements

Below are some of the things I am most proud of accomplishing.

Senior Honours Project

In my fourth year at university I carried out a research project about high redshift quiescent (or passive) galaxies. As part of this project, I had to write a report and create a poster. For this I achieved a first (80%).

Class Medal and Scholarship

For my academic achievement in Third Year I was awarded the Astrophysics Junior Honours Class Medal and the Nichol Foundation Scholarship for attaining the top mark (86%) across my year on all physics degree programmes.

Creating an online study space

At the beginning of the 2020/21 academic year, all teaching was set to be online, with limited access to campus and seeing other students. I took it upon myself to create a Discord Server where physics students could talk, ask for help, and compare solutions as we would normally. It's still in use at the time of writing (middle of 2022), and over 300 people have joined.
